The Turner Realty Story

Since 1998, we've built an award-winning brokerage on integrity, innovation, and community commitment. Here's how we got here—and where we're going.

From One Office to a Province-Wide Network

Royal LePage Turner Realty was founded in 1998 by Gaye Turner in Gander, Newfoundland. What started as a single broker with a vision has grown into a respected brokerage operating three offices across the province with 11 licensed REALTORS® serving thousands of clients.

Throughout our growth, we've never lost sight of our founding values: integrity, hard work, and an unwavering commitment to client success. Every award we've won, every achievement we've celebrated, has been earned through the dedication of our agents and our investment in cutting-edge technology.
