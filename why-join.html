Questions from licensed agents

What REALTORS® ask us before joining

Straight answers on splits, licence transfers, technology, training, and what it's actually like working Labrador West from a Gander-based brokerage.

What's the commission split at Royal LePage Turner Realty?

Our splits are competitive with the strongest Atlantic Canada brokerages and scale with your production. Rather than publish a generic grid, we discuss splits, caps, and any desk-fee structure one-on-one so we can tailor them to your volume, business plan, and how much mentorship or independence you want. Book a confidential conversation with Mike Turner, Broker of Record, to see real numbers for your situation — get in touch.

How do I transfer my real estate licence to Royal LePage in Labrador West?

Transferring your NLAR licence is straightforward. You submit a Transfer of Registration form through the Newfoundland and Labrador Association of REALTORS®, and we handle the brokerage side — sponsoring-broker paperwork, MLS/Matrix reassignment, rlpSPHERE CRM onboarding, and Royal LePage national-systems access. Most transfers complete in three to five business days with no gap in your ability to list or sell.

Do I need to move to Gander or work out of a physical office?

No. Labrador West agents work remotely from Labrador City or Wabush. Our Gander HQ at 204 Airport Boulevard handles administrative support, compliance, trust accounting, and broker supervision — you don't commute there. You list, show, and close locally; we handle the back office.

What does rlpSPHERE CRM actually include?

rlpSPHERE is Royal LePage's proprietary Smart CRM — contact management, automated drip campaigns, listing presentations, transaction management, and lead routing, all integrated in one platform. It also connects to Smart Leads (national lead distribution from royallepage.ca), automated market reports, Marketing Autopilot, and the 20,000+ REALTOR® Royal LePage referral network across Canada. There's no separate monthly tech fee.

What training and mentorship is included?

Every Turner agent gets rlpCAMPUS self-directed online training, weekly live webinars, Buffini & Company coaching, Chris Leader's Peak Producers program, and twice-yearly in-person Marketing Workshops. You also get direct mentorship from Mike Turner (Broker of Record since 1998) and our Chairman's Club Top 1% team. Onboarding is tailored to where you are — new licensee or experienced producer.

Is Labrador West a big enough market to build a real estate career?

Yes. Labrador City and Wabush have a combined population of roughly 10,000, anchored by one of North America's largest iron-ore mining operations (IOC/Rio Tinto and Tacora Resources). Mining-driven employment creates consistent housing turnover — engineers, trades, and management relocate in and out on multi-year cycles. It's an under-served MLS market with real room for a focused agent to dominate, and Royal LePage Turner brings the brand, technology, and leads to compete on day one. See the Labrador West market overview for the numbers.
